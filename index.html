How to Play

  • Place the phone so your full body is visible from the side.
  • Wait for the readiness light to turn green before starting.
  • Single Player: clear the rep target to progress to the next stage.
  • Battle Mode: Quick Match auto-finds an opponent, or use Private Room for a friend.
  • Every 5 squats gives you 1 attack charge. Raise both hands to strike, and squat during danger to block.
